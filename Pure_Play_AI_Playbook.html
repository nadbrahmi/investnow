What is a Pure-Play AI Stock?

A "pure-play" AI stock derives the majority of its revenue directly from AI software, chips, or infrastructure — unlike Mag 7 giants (Apple, Google, Meta, etc.) where AI is one feature among many. Pure-plays offer higher AI upside but also higher concentration risk.

P/E Gap Explained

P/E ratio = Stock Price ÷ Earnings Per Share. Pure-play AI stocks command sky-high P/E ratios because investors are paying for FUTURE AI dominance, not current profits. The Mag 7 trade at 25–38× — pure-plays trade at 100–260×. That gap is the "AI premium" you're paying.

Risk Dimensions Explained

Valuation: How stretched is the stock price vs earnings? Higher = more expensive. Concentration: How dependent is revenue on AI or a single customer/segment? Execution: Can management deliver on promises? Macro: How sensitive is the stock to interest rates, tariffs, and recession risk?
