How to read analyst targets

Analyst average targets are the median of all Wall Street 12-month price forecasts. Implied upside = how much the stock would need to rise to hit the average target. High upside often means the stock has been sold off hard — not always a buy signal. Always combine with fundamentals and risk score.

AI Infrastructure vs AI Security — What's the difference?

AI Infrastructure = the physical/network layer powering AI (servers, networking, power, data centers). AI Security = software protecting AI systems, cloud workloads, and identities from attacks. Infrastructure is more cyclical; security offers more recurring subscription revenue.
